# Audience

- who: Denver-metro homeowners (primary), small commercial (secondary). Mostly own older + mid-century homes.
- jobs_to_be_done: "fix an urgent failure fast", "upgrade safely & to code", "capture rebates/financing".
- objections: price, trust ("will they upsell?"), permit hassle, scheduling.
- intent_mix: ~60% transactional/emergency, ~40% research-before-buy.
- what builds trust: licensing, real reviews, upfront pricing, local neighborhood knowledge.
