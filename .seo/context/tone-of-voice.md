# Tone of Voice

- voice: authoritative but neighborly; expert tradesperson who explains plainly.
- reading_level: 8th–9th grade.
- do: concrete local detail (neighborhoods, codes, climate), upfront about cost ranges, short paragraphs.
- dont: hype superlatives without proof, fake urgency, jargon walls, "Lorem ipsum", filler.
- banned_words: ["unparalleled","world-class","cutting-edge","synergy","game-changer"]
- cta_style: clear and direct — call, text, or book; one primary CTA per section.
